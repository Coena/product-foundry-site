Our values

Everyone works to become a leader, practitioner, and learner.

Every individual in our company, from the founders to the most recent member of our team, is expected to become an industry-wide thought and practice leader in a discipline to the extent they can teach others within and outside the company about the practice. At the same time, the same individual is expected to work as a producing expert on analysis, designs, or development in a consulting engagement, venture, or an open source initiative. Finally, the same individual is expected to be learning something they are completely unfamiliar with, either by engaging in a formal course or degree, or by seeking a mentor within or outside the company.

We prefer working with people who play a long game.

It's often tempting to chase a fleeting opportunity and compromise our culture. We don't like tackling “low-hanging fruit” if it means compromising our objectives, and we prefer working with people who stick to their principles despite the promise of immediate bounty.

We're small and global by choice.

We operate in multiple locations around the world, each location containing no more than 8 people. We believe the best teams are small teams that are inter-cultural, and because we develop distributed, disruptive, and discontinuous solutions, we are a distributed, disruptive, and discontinuous organization by choice.

High-hanging fruit

Businesses today face unprecedented complexity in their markets and organisations. The majority of businesses ignore this complexity by reducing it to quick wins with low-hanging fruit, which neither differentiates them nor makes them competitive. We help exceptional and driven businesses identify, create, and sustain “high-hanging fruit” that makes their market-facing solutions differentiating and market-winning, while keeping their organisation highly reactive to change.

What we do

Agile Design

Create systems that embrace existing forces, shed non-essential and historical complexity, and empower those with an entrepreneurial spirit.

We operate from a deep conviction that today's business landscape needs profound change. We apply Embodied Making, the leading agile design method to effect this change. We work with clients ranging from large corporates to start-ups, entrepreneurs, and independent consultants who share our values. We help transform demand-supply driven project structures into product organizations where it's everyone's job to create, sustain, and re-create products, and no one's job to solely monitor and supervise others.

Kick-ass Projects

Why can't business applications be fun-to-use and well-built like some of the best games are?

There are online games that give real-time interactive experiences to a few hundred thousand online users, and somehow CRM or ERP applications are still struggling to create or change order-data for a few thousand users for greater expense and poorer efficiency.

We feel business applications need to be built in better ways, and we're proud to be part of that change. We offer development services for creating web and mobile products by balancing the needs of the market with leading technology. We are industry-leaders in developing real-time and reactive applications, and we are proud to have industry thought-leaders as our clients.

Product Development

We use our own management consulting and product development practices to develop our own products.

Together with our implementation partners, we are currently working in innovations for sensemaking, telecommunications, retail, and logistics.

Management Consulting

We offer management consulting services for Enterprise Transformation and Innovation using Embodied Making.

We prefer working as integral team members with the teams of our clients to co-create change. We actively work independent management consultants as an extended part of our organization.
