Our values

Everyone works to become a leader, practitioner, and learner.

Every individual in our company, from the founders to the most recent member of our team, is expected to become an industry-wide thought and practice leader in a discipline to the extent they can teach others within and outside the company about the practice. At the same time, the same individual is expected to work as a producing expert on analysis, designs, or development in a consulting engagement, venture, or an open source initiative. Finally, the same individual is expected to be learning something they are completely unfamiliar with, either by engaging in a formal course or degree, or by seeking a mentor within or outside the company.

We prefer working with people who play a long game.

It's often tempting to chase a fleeting opportunity and compromise our culture. We don't like tackling “low-hanging fruit” if it means compromising our objectives, and we prefer working with people who stick to their principles despite the promise of immediate bounty.

We're small and global by choice.

We operate in multiple locations around the world, each location containing no more than 8 people. We believe the best teams are small teams that are inter-cultural, and because we develop distributed, disruptive, and discontinuous solutions, we are a distributed, disruptive, and discontinuous organization by choice.

High-hanging fruit

Businesses today face unprecedented complexity in their markets and organisations. The majority of businesses ignore this complexity by reducing it to quick wins with low-hanging fruit, which neither differentiates them nor makes them competitive. We help exceptional and driven businesses identify, create, and sustain “high-hanging fruit” that makes their market-facing solutions differentiating and market-winning, while keeping their organisation highly reactive to change.

Profound change

We operate from a deep conviction that today's business landscape needs profound change, and we structure all of our work in terms of people, planet and profit. The method we apply to effect profound change is called embodied making, the leading agile design method. We work with clients (both corporates and start-ups), entrepreneurs, and independent consultants who share these values. For our clients, we help them move away from demand-supply projects to creating product organizations where it's everyone's job to create, sustain, and re-create products, and no one's job to solely monitor and supervise others. In our social ventures, we work to create systems that embrace existing forces, shed non-essential and historical complexity, and empower those with an entrepreneurial spirit. And in our open source initiatives, we work to create the foundations of a real-time business.

What we do

Research

We dedicate up to 40 percent of our time to research, from publishing papers in leading journals to open writing.

Our research fuels our work, our work feeds our research

Up to 40% of our time is spent on research, financed by the space created by our commercial engagements and ventures. From recording our analysis to publishing papers, from working on open source projects to ventures, from engaging in courseware to doing part-time degrees, research forms a core part of our purpose. We proudly stand on the shoulders of giants.

Practice

We work for enterprises hungry for profound change, with start-ups that live the same values we do, and on our own social ventures.

Method

Together with our co-creators, we take the results of our research and practice by contributing to Embodied Making.

About Embodied Making

Embodied making is a reflective and recursive practice of progressively understanding situations where problems occur through deep enquiry, finding elegant solutions for those problems, applying those solutions cautiously to see how environments reshaped around them, and then starting the process again by understanding how solutions have created new situations through deep enquiry.
